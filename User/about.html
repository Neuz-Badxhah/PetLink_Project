System Purpose

Our platform aims to facilitate the adoption and sale of pets in a user-friendly environment. We strive to connect pet lovers with reputable sellers and provide comprehensive information about pets available for adoption or purchase.

Management of the System

The system is managed by a dedicated team committed to ensuring smooth operations and excellent user experience. Our administrators oversee platform functionality, handle user queries, and maintain the integrity of listings to ensure trustworthiness and reliability.

Roles

Our platform supports multiple user roles to ensure smooth operation:

  • Admin: Manages the platform, ensures all operations are running smoothly, and handles user queries.
  • Seller: Posts listings of pets available for adoption or sale.
  • User: Browses listings and contacts sellers for pets they are interested in.
